Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Vectrail Knot, a single-player offline puzzle game.

Data Collection

Vectrail Knot does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data. The game does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

Offline Gameplay

Vectrail Knot is designed to work offline. The game does not require an internet connection for gameplay, levels, progress, music, or settings.

Local Progress

The game may save progress, stars, level unlock state, sound settings, and music settings locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or to any third party.

Analytics and Tracking

Vectrail Knot does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection tools.

Advertising and In-App Purchases

Vectrail Knot does not include third-party advertising and does not require in-app purchases to play.

Children's Privacy

Because Vectrail Knot does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If the app changes in a way that affects privacy, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
